# 📖 StudyFlow

> Track your study sessions, monitor your performance, and build a consistent study habit — all in one place.

**[🚀 Live Demo](https://pedrocosta28228-maker.github.io/study-tracker/)**

---

## ✨ Features

- **⏱ Study Timer** — Stopwatch and countdown timer to track your study sessions in real time
- **📊 Performance Dashboard** — Visualize your total hours, questions answered, correct answers, errors and success rate
- **🔥 Streak System** — Stay motivated! Study for 5+ consecutive days and earn a fire streak badge
- **📋 Study Plans** — Create custom study plans with multiple subjects and organize your routine
- **📅 Consistency Tracker** — A visual day-by-day bar showing which days you studied (≥20 min counts)
- **🗂 Session History** — Full log of all your study sessions by subject and topic
- **💾 Local Storage** — All your data is saved automatically in your browser — no account needed

---

## 📌 How to Use

1. Click **"Registrar Estudo"** to log a past study session
2. Or click **"Estude agora!"** to start the timer and log when you finish
3. Select your subject, add the topic, and fill in questions answered
4. Watch your stats update automatically on the dashboard
5. Keep studying daily to build your 🔥 streak!

## 🇧🇷 Sobre o Projeto

**StudyFlow** é uma ferramenta gratuita para estudantes que querem organizar e acompanhar sua rotina de estudos. Registre suas sessões, veja seu desempenho por matéria, e mantenha a constância com o sistema de streak — que te premia com um 🔥 após 5 dias seguidos de estudo.

Desenvolvido com HTML, CSS e JavaScript puro, sem necessidade de instalação. Basta abrir no navegador e começar a estudar.
